Why Homeopathy Makes Sense and Works.

The word “homeopathy” is derived from two Greek words: homoios which means “similar” and pathoswhich means “suffering.” Homeopathy's basic premise is called the “principle of similars,” and it refers to recurrent observation and experience that a medicinal substance will elicit a healing response for the specific syndrome of symptoms (or suffering) that it has been proven to cause when given in overdose to a healthy person.
